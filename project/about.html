What is Fika?

Fika is a cherished Swedish tradition that means taking a break for coffee and a pastry. But it's much more than just a coffee break; it's a state of mind, a moment to slow down and appreciate the good things in life with friends, family, or colleagues.

The word "fika" is both a noun and a verb. You can have a fika (a coffee and cake break), or you can fika (take a break). It's an integral part of Swedish culture, and you'll find cafes bustling with people enjoying fika at all times of the day.

This site is dedicated to helping you find the perfect spot for your next fika in Gothenburg, a city renowned for its cozy and unique cafes.
