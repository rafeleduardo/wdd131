Site Plan: Fika Göteborg

1. Site Name

Fika Göteborg

Domain: fikagoteborg.se

The name is simple, memorable, and uses a key Swedish term, making it easily recognizable for both locals and tourists. The proposed domain is professional and geographically relevant.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of Fika Göteborg is to be a curated, personal guide to the best independent and unique cafes in Gothenburg. It will help users discover authentic fika experiences beyond the major chains, focusing on atmosphere, quality of pastries, and coffee.

3. Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A tourist visiting the city wants to experience a genuine Swedish fika. They find the site and use it to locate a cozy, highly-recommended cafe near their location in the city center.
  • Scenario 2: A local resident is looking for a new cafe with a relaxing atmosphere to meet a friend. They browse the site to find a unique spot in a different neighborhood that is known for its excellent cinnamon buns.

4. Color Schema

The color palette will be warm and inviting, inspired by coffee, pastries, and cozy interiors. The colors have been checked for WCAG AA compliance.

  • Primary/Accent: A warm, earthy brown (#8B4513) for headings and highlights.
  • Background: A soft, creamy off-white (#FAF3E6) that is easy on the eyes.
  • Text: A dark, rich grey (#333333) for readability.
  • Card Background: A clean, simple white (#FFFFFF) to make the cafe images and details stand out.

5. Typography

  • Headings: Playfair Display (from Google Fonts). An elegant, high-contrast serif that feels both classic and stylish.
  • Body Text: Lato (from Google Fonts). A friendly and highly readable sans-serif that works wonderfully for descriptions and details.
